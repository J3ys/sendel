The Problem

Finding out who wrote a specific section in Confluence is tedious. You have to open page history, click through multiple versions, and manually compare changes. It wastes time and makes accountability difficult.

The Solution — Just Blame

Just Blame adds inline author attribution to every Confluence page. Click one button and see who wrote what, highlighted with color-coded sections. Get answers in seconds, not minutes.

Key Features

  • Inline Attribution: See author names and timestamps directly on the page content.
  • Color-Coded Visualization: Different colors for different contributors make scanning easy.
  • Granular Tracking: Track authorship at paragraph, table, heading, or section level.
  • One-Click Activation: Toggle blame view on and off instantly.
  • Timeline View: Understand how your page evolved over time.
  • Zero Configuration: Works immediately after installation.
  • Permission Aware: Respects all Confluence security settings.
